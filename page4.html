Onoto Watanna (1903)

The Website

This website will showcase the author Onoto Watanna (Winnifred Eaton). She was an author who published fiction novels and articles, cookbooks, and wrote a memoir. Some of her most famous publications include Miss Numè of Japan (1899), Japanese Nightingale (1900), and Chinese-Japanese Cookbook (1914). Many of her fictional literary works focus on interracial relationships and perspectives, which stems from Watanna a having British-Chinese heritage.
