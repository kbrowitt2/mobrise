About the Website

This website will showcase the author Onoto Watanna (Winnifred Eaton). She was an author who published fiction novels and articles, cookbooks, and wrote a memoir. Some of her most famous publications include Miss Numè of Japan (1899), Japanese Nightingale (1900), and Chinese-Japanese Cookbook (1914). You can find links to her works under the publications tab.
